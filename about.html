Susan R. Zandhuis


DECOBAY Interiors LLC CEO | Principal Interior Designer


With two decades of experience in the design industry, Susan is the Founder & Principal Designer who brings a refined balance of creativity, technical knowledge, and practical expertise to every project. Her passion for design is matched by her commitment to precision and functionality. Currently pursuing her second degree in Architecture at the Academy of Art University, Susan seamlessly blends interior design with architectural insight, offering her clients a comprehensive design experience. Susan’s career has spanned luxury residential properties, commercial spaces, and bespoke projects, always guided by her core belief: that thoughtful design transforms not only spaces but lives.

DECOBAY Interiors LLC creates timeless interiors that blend modern aesthetics with classic elegance. Our approach is deeply personal, tailoring each space to reflect our clients' unique stories.
